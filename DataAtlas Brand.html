DataAtlas

A stacked-chevron mark for the Jira-native governance platform. Three upward chevrons, cool→warm. The pale top reads as "surface," the cyan middle as "core," the violet base as "depth" — a small visual story about layered data.

09

Don'ts

The mark is system-built — don't redraw it
Don't · fill
The chevrons are stroked. Don't fill the paths or thicken them.
Don't · invert order
Pale → cyan → violet, top to bottom. Inverting reads as "warning" or error.
Don't · saturated tile
Tile stays tonal/neutral. Don't put the mark on a brand color.
Don't · stretch
Tile is always square. Don't crop, stretch, or letterbox.
Don't · shadow
No glows, drop shadows, bevels, or 3D effects on the mark or tile.
Don't · decorate
section break
The chevron paths are a brand mark. Don't tile, repeat, or use as a divider.
