Product overview

A governed data workspace inside your Jira project

Data Atlas turns a Jira project into a complete governance operating model — catalogue, stewardship, intake, lineage, and admin in one place.

Governance Dashboard

Business Glossary

One catalogue for every term, KPI, and report

Give business users a clear place to find trusted definitions, measures, reports, and data context — without leaving Jira. Data Atlas makes the glossary a live, governed workspace, not a static document.

  • Business terms, KPIs, systems, policies, and information flows
  • Searchable by type, domain, owner, platform, and status
  • Definitions carry ownership and review dates
  • Available to all Jira users without admin access
Asset Register

Filter and manage the full asset inventory

Stewards get a focused, filterable view of every governed asset across the organisation. Slice by type, owner, classification, domain, platform, and status to find gaps and prioritise work.

  • Filter by asset type, classification, domain, and lifecycle status
  • Track business owner, data steward, and technical owner
  • Asset detail keeps context, ownership, and relationships in one Jira issue
  • Uses Jira fields, filters, and queues — no new UI to learn
Stewardship workflow

Move from passive documentation to active governance work

Data Atlas turns governance into trackable, triageable work — with review queues, ownership gaps, and Jira history so stewards always know what needs attention.

The review queue gives data stewards a focused view of assets that need review, ownership, or classification attention.

Requests from the portal or service project are routed into Data Atlas so stewards can triage new metrics, glossary terms, reports, and asset changes.

Each governed asset keeps context, ownership, lifecycle, and linked relationships in the same Jira work item.

Intake Portal

Capture data requests through a branded service portal

Capture requests for new measures, terms, dashboards, and asset changes through a branded Jira Service Management portal. Every request routes directly into the Data Atlas review and stewardship workflow.

  • New business terms, KPIs, reports, and systems
  • Asset changes and governance amendments
  • Routes directly into Data Atlas queues
  • Works with existing JSM portals and request types
Lineage & Context

Understand how assets connect

Data Atlas records how business concepts, systems, information flows, reports, and policies connect to each other — so stewards can trace impact, upstream sources, and governed relationships across the data landscape.

  • Sourced from, feeds into, derived from, governed by, reports on
  • Integration platforms modelled as systems and information flows
  • Impact graph shows downstream and upstream dependencies
  • Relationships preserved through import/export round-trips
Atlassian-Native Administration

Configure without leaving Jira

Configure governance domains, classifications, field visibility, saved views, and setup health directly from Data Atlas settings — all within your existing Jira project, with no extra admin tools required.

  • Governance domains: Customer Experience, Finance, Operations, Risk & Compliance, and more
  • Custom classification levels: Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted
  • Role-based access control with Jira-native permissions
  • Runs on Atlassian Forge — no external infrastructure
